PEICOSY UI TRANSFORMATION PROMPT (PASTE AS-IS)

Transform the entire PEICOSY app UI into a high-end luxury digital experience inspired by global fashion houses (Dior, Jacquemus, Net-a-Porter, Farfetch Private Client).

This is not a startup app and not a generic ecommerce UI. The brand targets elite, fashion-forward users in London and Johannesburg who expect restraint, confidence, and polish.

BRAND & MOOD

Aesthetic: Modern luxury, feminine power, quiet confidence

Emotion: Exclusivity, intimacy, control, desire

Tone: Minimal words, strong hierarchy, intentional whitespace

COLOR PALETTE

Primary background: Deep charcoal / midnight navy (#0B0F1A range)

Accent color: Signature luxury pink (used sparingly for emphasis only)

Text: Soft white / muted greys (never pure white)

Avoid bright colors, gradients, or playful tones

TYPOGRAPHY

Headlines: High-fashion serif (Didot / Playfair Display / Canela-style)

Body text: Clean modern sans-serif (Inter / Helvetica-style)

Strong contrast between headlines and body text

Headlines feel editorial, not tech

LAYOUT & SPACING

Generous margins and padding (luxury breathes)

Clear vertical rhythm

No clutter, no dense blocks

Content should feel curated, not crowded

BUTTONS & INTERACTIONS

Rounded, elegant buttons (not sharp, not cartoonish)

Subtle hover animations (fade, scale 1–2%)

No loud shadows or gimmicks

Primary CTA in pink, secondary in outline or muted tones

NAVIGATION

Clean top navigation

Minimal icons

Smooth transitions

Login and Shop feel discreet, premium, and intentional

HERO SECTION

Large editorial headline

Strong contrast between background and accent text

Headline feels like a fashion campaign, not marketing copy

Subtle motion only (fade-in, parallax scroll)

FORBIDDEN

No material design look

No default Replit UI components

No bright blues or greens

No excessive animations

No “startup” aesthetic

GOAL

The final UI should feel like:

A private members luxury platform

A fashion house digital experience

Something a high-net-worth woman would trust immediately

Make the app feel expensive, restrained, and timeless.
